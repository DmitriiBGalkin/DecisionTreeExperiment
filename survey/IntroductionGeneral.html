This study is part of the Transparency of Algorithmic Decision-Making Systems (TrADeMaS) project, conducted by Zeppelin University and the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI).

This experiment focuses on understanding decision trees. Decision trees are simple models that help make decisions based on rules.

The experiment consists of the following phases:

  1. Introduction: A short explanation of what decision trees are and how they work.
  2. Practice Session: Example questions to get familiar with the task.
  3. Main Study: Questions based on different decision trees.
  4. Demographics & Feedback: A short survey.
